Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do we do?

    VTA is a comprehensive career development platform that empowers individuals to achieve their full potential. We offer a range of services including AI-powered job matching, personalized learning paths, expert mentorship, and skill development assessments. Whether you're a job seeker looking to advance your career, a recruiter seeking top talent, an SME sharing expertise, or an institution focused on student success, VTA has the tools and resources to help you reach your goals.

  • What courses are covered in VTA?

    VTA offers a diverse range of courses covering various in-demand skills and industries. Our courses are curated based on industry trends and job market demands. You can find courses in areas such as programming, data science, digital marketing, design, finance, and more. We partner with industry-leading experts and institutions to deliver high-quality and up-to-date content.

  • How does the VTA courses help job seekers and recruiters on finding the best fit?

    The courses are designed to bridge the gap between academia and the workplace. By acquiring in-demand skills through our platform, job seekers can enhance their resumes, stand out to recruiters, and increase their employability. For recruiters, these courses provide a pool of skilled candidates with certified competencies, making the hiring process more efficient and effective.

  • How SME support can help the students/job seekers?

    Our SME network offers invaluable guidance and mentorship to students and job seekers. SMEs provide personalized career advice, share industry insights, and offer support on specific projects or challenges. By connecting with SMEs, users can gain a deeper understanding of their desired industry and accelerate their career progression.
